May the tears keep coming

As they clean the valves of our soul

May our voices never wither (voices)

In the abysm of the unknown

For with them we lose our sleep

And awaken our dreams

May the image you are seeking dissolve

So you can meet the real love that is you

So you can live the life that’s been awaiting

Your eyes to open your heart to roar
