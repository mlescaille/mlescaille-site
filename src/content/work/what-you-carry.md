You are the darker parts  
And the light inside  
You are determined for the anger inside as much as the joy  
You are your silences as much as your voice  
The time you choose to shine defines you as much as the time you hide  
The shame you carry is as deeply you  
As the pride that holds your head high  
Love and hate are so close  
It defines a moment:  
what you let go  
what you carry.
