*I think the most important question facing humanity is, 'Is the universe a friendly place? ' This is the first and most basic question all people must answer for themselves.*

***Albert Einstein***

Life is giving you life. I don’t see a reason why life wouldn’t want you to be good to be better to evolve, grow and get out of your own mind and entanglements. Life’s whispers do not come from mind.

**Lessons for 2021**

- Family lesson: compassion - seeing them for who they are as person, not the archetype of the rol of mother, dad, grandma
- Self - ego lessons: edge got out, smallness, mirror of your inner self reflected in the world
- Spiritual: If you expect to receive blessings from the heavens, then act and behave like someone who’s after blessings.
- In rest and easiness I can honor myself too. We get what we want
