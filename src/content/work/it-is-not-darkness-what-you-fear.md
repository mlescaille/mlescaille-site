You lifted my fears and I ascended  
You said fear no more, I’m here, and my heart softened  
You said how can you fear your own? Look around there is nothing to be afraid of  
The life I took for you was to build your strength.  
Now tell me are you going to accept more or less life?

You said to wait first, to act next and then you said expect!  
How can you not, you’ve been reassured.  
You said you have found the universe and then turned back on its mystery.  
Have you seen a bird flying against the wind turn back?  
Because it knows that its destiny is in the opposite direction it goes there.  
Nothing else  
Does the tree loses leaves in summer? Does it bring flowers in the dead of winter?  
I say the blueprint is in my heart. Only on arrival, only when the door of the deep within it  
Cracks open, only then. And we have begin our quest.  
You lifted my fears and I can see the moon side of my soul.
