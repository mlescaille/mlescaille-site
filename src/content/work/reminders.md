That my life is a practice  
That I can do hard things  
That I can feel  
That there’s no end goal  
That I can be kind and generous to myself too  
That expression is our right in this world  
That those who changed us don’t get to define who we become  
That forgiveness is freedom  
Of my energy and my heart to be open again  
That love is peace and the war is within
