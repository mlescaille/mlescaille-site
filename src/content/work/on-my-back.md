The cut in my back is letting the butterflies out.  
My spine is exposed to the elements and  
tumbling along they go  
One is red, crimson, one is black like ebony  
one is bright yellow, one is purple  
Like the flower in the vase on the nightstand.  
The butterflies have no blood in them  
How can they leave the wound fresh, no paint but their own?
