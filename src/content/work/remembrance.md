** **Thankful for the day  
And the moon stations  
Thankful for the hunger  
Thankful for family  
Raising from their own joys and darkness  
Building life through the red cord to my roots  
Thankful for the other family  
A new mom, dad and siblings  
Thankful for the gaze at other lands which is our land  
Thankful for the name changing at each step  
Baby, girl, woman  
Thankful for the challenges that elevate us to our gifts  
Always, always thankful for the gift.  
Thanks to the friendships that stayed and the ones that left.  
In purpose, thankful, lifted.

*Marisniulkis Lescaille*
