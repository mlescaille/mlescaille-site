Mentorship.

If you happen to have as new goal or resolution in 2017 helping people, I am looking for a mentor. Likewise, if you feel like you are the one in need, reach out to me.

> [](https://twitter.com/amyhoy/status/812297012959969280)

I have been thinking for a while to ask for guidance. I am mostly an introvert, it felt a lot awkward to ask for mentorship openly on the internet. But as I am building professionally I thought it is a good way to give it a shot.

My intention is to find a mentor for setting career goals, dealing with challenges and what applies most to my challenge right now, how to acquire new skills or even a new path while working as software engineer. If you have a middle way in business, that’s good too, I would like that kind of interaction as well.

And who knows, maybe I can provide something for you to learn too!

If you think you might do this, but you just not sure if I would be a good fit, we can try! Shoot me an email and we can arrange a call and get to know each other. After all, a mentorship is a *relationship* so some sort of connection must exist.
