Reset the pathways of the boards and chips as if they have lost track of the way they are supposed to go

As if they gathered dust and fog along the way muddling the goals set.

And so restart brings it all back to the initial point where everything was clear and the path is known.

Like that some people are meant to be the restart button of your heart.

Like that some people come and reset your pathways and clear the fog to ignite the flame you have forgotten.

Let it be as it is and do not ask more for life is sending you the gifts in small packages.
